Welcome to My Crochet Website

Hi! I'm Sophia, and I'm a DS4200 student at Northeastern, and when I am not studying, I really love to crochet. I have made a variety of different types of projects over the years This website is dedicated to showcasing my previous crochet projects. If you are interested in learning how to crochet, I will also link my contacts down below! I can also direct you to sources online that might be helpful when starting out with crochet/knitting. I know for me personally; I was able to learn best when I saw people showing proper hand posture and tension gauges on the yarn, so YouTube videos were most helpful for me. Once I got the hang of things I was able to read patterns that use shorthand and sometimes even symbols to denote stitches especially when you can get the more advanced projects like crochet in the round and when making clothes that have specific size requirements/measurements. For projects like the ones that are shown on the next page I recommend getting some extra equipment that can make the process go a lot smoother like stitch markers and plastic eye beads for any amigurumi projects.
